Terms & Conditions — Spam Mail Detector

Privacy & Read-Only Guarantee

Spam Mail Detector operates strictly in Read-Only Mode via IMAP protocol. We do not store your raw email contents or passwords permanently on any database.

1. Read-Only Access & Data Handling

When connecting an inbox, Spam Mail Detector connects exclusively via SSL/TLS IMAP in Read-Only state (`readonly=True`). The software processes message subjects and body tokens for real-time Neural Network inference. No email message is ever deleted, moved, modified, or forwarded.

2. User Privacy & Session Isolation

All analyzed emails and metrics are stored in temporary, session-isolated server memory (`SESSION_STORE`). Data is cleared immediately when you click Disconnect or close your browser session. Shared URLs or links opened in separate browsers will never expose past session data.

3. App Passwords & Security

Users are strongly advised to generate dedicated, removable 16-character App Passwords (rather than primary account passwords). Passwords are used solely during socket authentication and are never stored in database records.

4. Limitation of Liability

Spam Mail Detector is an Artificial Intelligence classification tool designed for educational and analytical purposes. While our Multi-Layer Perceptron (ANN) model achieves high accuracy, no AI filter can guarantee 100% classification error immunity. The developers are not liable for misclassified messages.
